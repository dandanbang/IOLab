Here you have a way to add tracks from the 'Search Result' list to the 'On the Go' list'

When you find the track you are searching, just add it to your 'On the Go' playlist with the 'Add On the Go' button. If you feel like changing the order the tracks are displayed, change it with the 'Up' and 'Down' buttons.

Play each track with the 'Play button and enjoy your MUSIC list making!
