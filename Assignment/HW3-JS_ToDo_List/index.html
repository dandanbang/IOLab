Hello Mr. HW4 -- To Do List

Here you have a way to add things to your "To Do" list by adding and submitting text using the "Add Item" button. Items will automatically appear in your "To Do" list. When you have completed any task, click the "Move Me" button to send your item on over to the "Completed Items" list. Similarly, you can use the respective button to move your item back to the "To Do" list. Enjoy your list making.
