To-Do List

Step 1: To start adding tasks onto your To-Do List, please input a task in the textbox and click the pink "Add New Item" button or hit the "return" key.

Note: Feel free to rearrange items in the list by dragging and dropping them anywhere within your list.

Step 2: Once you complete a task, click the button next to the associated task to move it to the "Completed" list.
