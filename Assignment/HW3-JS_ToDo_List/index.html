Your Online To-Do List

How to use this website: You can type your to-do item in the text input box below, and click "Add to To-do List" once you're done.

The item you've added will show up in the "To-do List" below. However, once you complete that item, you could move it to "Completed-item List" by clicking "Move to Completed-item List" button in that "To-do List".

Similarly, you could also unmark that completed item in your "Completed-item List" by clicking "Add to To-do List" button next to that item.
